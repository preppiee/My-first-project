What is Hyperpigmentation?

Hyperpigmentation occurs when certain areas of the skin become darker due to excess melanin production. This can be caused by sun exposure, hormonal changes, acne scars, or inflammation. It affects all skin types and is treatable with the right care.

Our program is specially designed to reduce the appearance of dark spots, even out your skin tone, and restore your skin's natural glow.
